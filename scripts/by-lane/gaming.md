## What changed (the "not an ad" fix)
- Each now passes the **delete test** — remove Threadverse and it's still a real story/value post.
- Product is **named ≤ once**, casually ("one feed I use", "the one I use now"), and **shown in frame** via screen-record — the setting, not the subject.
- The head fake is now the **idea/story twist**, not "…buy this app". Product is ambient.
- **CTA mostly dropped** from the script (link lives in bio). Two keep no CTA at all.
- ~60% story-first (02, 04, 07, 09, 10, +partial), ~40% value-first (01, 03, 05, 06, 08).
